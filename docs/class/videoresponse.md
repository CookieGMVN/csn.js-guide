## Properties

> ### .quality
The quality of video download link
>
**Type:** "180p" or "360p" or "480p" or "720p" or "1080p"

> ### .link
The video download link
>
**Type:** [String](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/JavaScript/Reference/Global_Objects/String)
